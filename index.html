Mission Statement

We are a community dedicated to serving the world through education and leadership. We are dedicated to three pillars of student competence: Community Service, Cultural Engagement, Citizen Leadership.

Coastal University upholds the tradition of building students of character by implementing the three pillars in everything we do. Every department at the university actively engages students to become of the highest moral, social, and intellectual order.

read more

Community Service

Students are challenged to not isolate themselves and seek out ways to be an active positive contribution to society. Students are presented with opportunities to become involved in the campus and state community.

Cultural Engagement

Students are challenged intellectually to become culturally competent and see the beauty in a plathora of different cultures. with the nations highest diversity rankings it is imperative that Coastal University be dedicated to understanding the struggles and success of many different cultural backgrounds.

Citizen Leadership

It is important for our students to be productive and positive members of society. This starts with an understanding of their civic responsibilities. We require all students to take two american government classes to understand how our country works as well as their rights and responsibilities as a citizen.
